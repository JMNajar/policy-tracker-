PMTA ROADMAP

FDA Premarket Tobacco Application — What to Know

The PMTA is the mandatory FDA approval pathway for any new nicotine or tobacco product. Understanding this process is critical for GFT1 device planning and investor communications.

PhaseWhat HappensTimelineCost Estimate
Pre-Submission Scientific literature review, product testing, safety & benefit studies, manufacturer audit 6–12 months $500K–$1.5M
Application Filing Submit full PMTA dossier to FDA CTP. Includes toxicology, clinical data, manufacturing specs Month 12–15 Included above
FDA Filing Review FDA confirms application is complete and accepted for substantive review (refuse-to-file risk) 3–6 months post-filing —
Substantive Review FDA evaluates whether product is "appropriate for the protection of public health" (APPH standard) 12–18 months $1.5M–$3.5M (consultant + legal)
Marketing Order FDA issues Marketing Granted Order (MGO) — product can be legally sold in the U.S. 18–24 months total $3–5M total
