Who Controls Cannabis Regulation

Congressional legislation is only half the picture. These executive agencies shape cannabis policy through rules, guidance, and enforcement — often faster than Congress can act.

AgencyCannabis AuthorityWhy It Matters for Business
DEAScheduling decisions — Schedule I vs. IIIThe single biggest regulatory event possible. Rescheduling changes banking, research, and legal risk overnight.
FDACannabis in food, supplements, and medicineDirectly governs Deeper Green™ (THC as a food ingredient). FDA rules determine legal B2B sales.
DOJ / AGFederal prosecution prioritiesCan deprioritize cannabis enforcement without Congress. The AG's stance determines real-world risk.
Treasury / FinCENBanking guidance for cannabis businessesFinCEN memos tell banks whether they can serve cannabis clients. Changes here affect payment processing.
USDAHemp farming regulations, Farm Bill implementationSets THC threshold for hemp vs. cannabis classification. Affects what can cross state lines legally.
