RISK SCORING

How We Score Bill Risk

Every bill in the live tracker carries a risk score: LOW MEDIUM HIGH CRITICAL

Scores are computed automatically at each weekly build using the bill's most recent action from Congress.gov. The scoring model weights signals as follows:

SignalWeight
Passed chamber / signed into law+5 points
Floor vote scheduled or recorded+3 points
Markup / ordered reported from committee+2 points
Hearing / subcommittee referral+1 point
Known high-priority bill (SAFER Banking, Schedule III, 280E, etc.)+2 points

A bill scoring 6+ is CRITICAL. 4–5 is HIGH. 2–3 is MEDIUM. 0–1 is LOW. Scores reset each weekly build based on the most current Congress.gov data.

CONFLICT FLAGS

How We Flag Conflicts of Interest

A ⚠ Conflict badge appears on a lawmaker's profile when two conditions are both true:

  1. The lawmaker sits on a committee with direct jurisdiction over cannabis legislation (Senate Judiciary, Senate Banking, House Judiciary, House Financial Services, Senate Finance, or Senate Agriculture)
  2. FEC records show the lawmaker's campaign received documented funding from industries with known financial incentives to oppose cannabis reform (pharmaceutical, alcohol, or private prison industries) during the 2023–2024 cycle

Conflict flags are based on documented FEC filings and publicly available committee rosters. All underlying data is verifiable at fec.gov and congress.gov.
